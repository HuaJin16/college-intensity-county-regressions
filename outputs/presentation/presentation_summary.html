County College Intensity - Presentation Summary

Cross-sectional county regressions for rent and wage outcomes (50 states + DC).

Generated: 2026-04-19 16:10

Executive Takeaways

  • Rent baseline: college intensity is positively associated with rent (beta=0.0016, p<0.001), about 0.16% higher rent per +1 pp college intensity.
  • Wage baseline: association with wage is near zero and statistically weak (beta=0.0001, p=0.814, ~0.01% per +1 pp).
  • Rent extensive/intensive results: the extensive-margin indicator is near zero once the two margins are separated, while the intensive-margin term remains positive and precise.
  • Wage extensive/intensive results: neither the extensive-margin indicator nor the pooled intensive-margin term is precise in the full-sample joint model.
  • Interpretation: estimates are associational (cross-sectional), not causal.
